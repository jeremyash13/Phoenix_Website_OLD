FAQ

Can I use my product/license key on more than one computer?

Yes, you can use your key on up to 5 devices simultaneously. Activating the key on a 6th device will automatically deactivate one of the 5 previously used devices.

Is my purchase valid for both Windows and MacOS?

Currently only Windows is supported, but support for MacOS is coming soon. Yes, you will be able to use your key on either Windows or MacOS devices.

Is my payment secure?

Yes, we use Stripe as our payment processor, one of the biggest names in the game.

I never received an email or product key, what do I do?

First, double check your spam folder. In rare cases it can take up to an hour to receive your email confirmation. If you still haven’t received your key within an hour, shoot us an email at: support@phoenixblocker.com

Phoenix isn’t what I expected, can I get a refund?

All sales are final. However, Phoenix is constantly improving and any feedback on we can further improve it is very much appreciated. Let us know your thoughts. Shoot us an email at feedback@phoenixblocker.com
